Top 10 Makeup Tips

Your signature guide to a flawless, magical glow every day.

1. Prep with Moisturizer

Always apply moisturizer before your primer to ensure a smooth, hydrated canvas.

2. Melt-Proof Primer

Match your primer to your skin needs—mattifying for oily areas, hydrating for dry spots.

3. Map Your Concealer

Apply concealer in an upside-down triangle shape under the eyes to instantly lift your face.

4. Warm Up Your Lash Curler

Blow warm air from your hairdryer onto your lash curler for a few seconds for an extra-lasting curl.

5. The Spoon Eyebrow Trick

Use the curved edge of a clean spoon to map out the perfect arch for your eyebrows.

6. Set T-Zone with Powder

Focus translucent powder primarily on your T-zone to reduce shine while leaving cheeks glowing.

7. Line and Fill Lips

Line your lips and fill them entirely with liner before lipstick to make your lip color stay all day.

8. Damp Beauty Sponge

Always wet your beauty sponge and squeeze out excess water before bouncing foundation into skin.

9. Blush Placement Matters

Apply blush higher up on the cheekbones rather than the apples for a sculpted, lifted appearance.

10. Lock It in with Setting Spray

Mist setting spray in an 'X' and 'T' motion across your face as the final step to melt powders together.
